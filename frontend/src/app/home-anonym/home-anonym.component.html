Easily group your shots

As soon as you import your data, TenniShots will break down your shots into a wide range of time intervals.

Detailed statistics for each period will be one click away from you thanks to TenniShots bueautiful interface.

Visualize trends

Wanna see how your forehand is progressing on a day-by-day basis? Or maybe how much spin your backhand has gained over the past year?

Whether you want to keep track of short-term or long-term trends TenniShots has got you covered.


Smart and social

Compete against yourself and your friends

Friends

Everything is more fun with friends! TenniShots lets you see the activity summary of your friends already using the service.

And if you like some friendly competition, you will love being able to see each other statistics side by side to determine who smashes that ball harder!

Tag and compare

We have a powerful tag system in place that will allow you to filter your sessions based on any criteria you choose.

For example, if you want to compare your match performance to your practice sessions, you will be able to do so in just a matter of seconds.

All your tennis videos have a new smart home

Upload your videos

You can upload any live mode recording you took through the sensor app.

TenniShots will automatically generate a video of your hitting session with statistics for each shots, and will remove any uneventful portion of the video.

Make your own highlights

TenniShots gives you the ability to filter through all the shots you recorded in just a few clicks.

Creating custom video clips with all your best shots is quick and simple!
