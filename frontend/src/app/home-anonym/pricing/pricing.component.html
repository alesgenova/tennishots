Pricing

Tennishots number crunching and video processing features require a sizeable amount of resources, and altough we would love it, we cannot offer the service for free.

Every new user can take advantage of a 7 day free trial.

Test all the features Tennishots has to offer and decide for yourself if you like the service (spoiler alert: you will). No credit card information is required!

We only ask for $1 every 1000 shots imported. We found that on average it corresponds to about $0.50 every hour of play.

Video processing and storage is very expensive, and we ask for an additional $2 every 1000 shots with video imported.
